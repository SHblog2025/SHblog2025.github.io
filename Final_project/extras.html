Spinoffs

Mayans M.C." is a crime drama that follows Ezekiel "EZ" Reyes, a prospect in the Mayans M.C. charter on the U.S.-Mexico border, two-and-a-half years after the events of Sons of Anarchy. EZ's journey is driven by a desire for revenge against those who wronged his family. The series explores his struggles with violence, identity, and the corrupting influence of the motorcycle club.
