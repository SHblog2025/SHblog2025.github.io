Main Characters

Maddie

Maddie is an averge girl just trying to get through high school like everyone else. She has her two best friends, Simon and Nicole, and her now ex-boyfriend Xavier. Maddie becomes a spirit and sets out on a mission to find out what happened to her.

Simon

Simon is Maddies best friend who secretly loves her. When Maddie becomes a spirit he is the only person that can see her.

Janet

Janet is a spirit that was working with Mr. Martin to cross over. She becomes overwhelmed and ends up taking Maddies body, leaving maddie as a spirit.

Mr. Martin

Mr. Martin is the teacher. He is the essential bad guy. He traps all the spirits in the high school while he tries to figure out how to steal someones body.

Wally

Wally died while playing on the football field. He becomes Maddie's new love intrest.

Charley

Charlie died from a peanut allergy. He becomes a good friend to Maddie

Rhonda

Rhonda was killed by her counselor. She's kind of dark and prudy.

Xavior

Xavior was Maddies boyfriend until he cheated on her.

Nicole

Nicole is Maddies other best friend.
