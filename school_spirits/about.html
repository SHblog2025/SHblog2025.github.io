About School Spirits

School Spirits is an awesome show that will keep you on the edge of your chair. Maddie gets body napped and becomes a spirit trapping her in her high school. She meets and becomes friends with other kids that died at the high school, Wally, Charley, and Rhonda. The go on a quest to find out what happened to Maddie and uncover lots of secrets that were being hid by Mr. Martin. Come enjoy the journey
